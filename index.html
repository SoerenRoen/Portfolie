What Can I Find?

This website is the portfolio for Søren Vindfelt Røn. It’s a started project for the multimedia design first semester examen, of KEA in the summer 2019.

The site will represent some of the skills, that Søren has acquired though the first semester of the education, and it will be the main base of the examen.

This site includes specific chosen projects, that the students has worked with through the semester. The projects are divided in 4 different categories, with each project put in to the right theme.

Through the projects, a lot of different skills will be represented, like HTML, CSS, JS, Design/Animation, and Videos.

In the about section, a short resume of Søren can be found. You can find both educations, relevant work experience, and the hobbies.

You can find a deeper description in the “About”-section, but the musical projects of Søren, can be found in the music section, sorted in different genres. Please notice, that the music shared on this site, is carefully sorted and chosen by Serbal, and there for, you might not find every track on this site.
