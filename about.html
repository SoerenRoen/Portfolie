About Me

My name is Søren Vindfelt Røn. I’m a young man from 1996, May, who lives in Copenhagen. I’ve been living here for the past year, since i moved from my parents house, in a small town called, Hareskov, which is located just outside Copenhagen.

At the moment I’m studying at KEA, education called Multimedia Design. It’s a very interesting education, that will optimize your skills as an graphic designer, and working with web design for a company, or other things. This site is the final project of the first semester, which includes specific chosen tasks, made through the year.

I have completed mid- and high-school, before i started at KEA. I went to a high school called Borupgaard Gymnasium, where I studiet Math, Music and Spanish at the highest level (A-niveau).

I chose Spanish, so that i could learn to talk with my uncle in his main language, and his family in Cuba.

In the music classes i learnt a lot of theory and history about music, which I will get back to later.

Through the years I’ve spent a lot of time working. I especially worked a long time for the company called JOE AND THE JUICE. I started out, as a normal employe, but got promoted to bar manager, which put me in charge of one of the stores in Denmark.

I was responsible for the growth in Index, the employes, the training and all the ordering of everything.

A little later, I got promoted again. I was asked if i wanted to join the concept development apartment, which included graphic designers, food development and the music management, where I was asked to join.

The job included, keeping track of all the different playlists, in every different store, world wide. I were also responsible for the production of music, to commercials and other things. I was even called in, as the DJ, at one of the big company events.

I’ve always loved music, and I’ve spent almost all my time listening og making music of my own. In an early age i started singing, and playing instruments, and it has been a hobby of mine, ever since.

When i got a little older, i got very much into DJ’ing, which kicked my into the production of my own music. At this moment, I think I’ve been producing music for about 6 years. There has been some jobs, where I’ve been booked to make some music for a company or some friends, but besides that, I’m just playing and singing for a good time.
